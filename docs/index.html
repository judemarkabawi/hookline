Hookline

Hookline is a 2D platformer where you use a grappling hook to move around a futuristic city. Your goal is to avoid hazards, figure out where to go, and use your grappling hook and boosts to get you up and to your goal.

Gameplay

Controls

  • W/A/D - control movement, stronger while grappling
  • Mouse click (hold) - attach and hold grapple
